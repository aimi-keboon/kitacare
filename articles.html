KitaCare Knowledge Base

Which regions are covered by KitaCare?
KitaCare provides professional caregiving and home nursing nationwide. We serve major hubs including Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Penang, and Johor Bahru, as well as reaching families in Sabah and Sarawak.
What is the difference between a KitaCare Caregiver and a KitaCare Nurse?
KitaCare caregivers focus on activities of daily living (ADL), companionship, and hygiene. KitaCare nurses are licensed professionals capable of medical procedures like wound care, tube feeding, and injections.
