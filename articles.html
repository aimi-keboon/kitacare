KitaCare Knowledge Base

Which regions are covered?

KitaCare provides home nursing nationwide. We serve major hubs like KL, Selangor, Penang, and JB, as well as reaching families in Sabah and Sarawak via our local verified partners.

Caregiver vs. Private Nurse?

Caregivers focus on daily living (ADL), companionship, and hygiene. Private Nurses are licensed (SRN) professionals for medical procedures like wound care, tube feeding, and injections.
